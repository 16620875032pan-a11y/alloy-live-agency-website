Creator support with a steady operating rhythm

How It Works

A clear agency support process

Step 1

Creator contacts Alloy or receives an official invitation.

Step 2

Alloy reviews the creator’s LIVE activity and eligibility.

Step 3

The creator completes the official onboarding process through TikTok.

Step 4

Alloy provides ongoing operational guidance and support.

All official agency invitations and onboarding steps are completed through TikTok’s official systems. Alloy does not request creator account passwords.

Safety and Trust

Clear boundaries for creator protection

Alloy keeps creator support transparent, practical, and aligned with official platform processes.

  • Alloy does not ask creators for TikTok passwords.
  • Alloy does not charge creators for standard agency support.
  • Official invitations are completed through TikTok’s official agency system.
  • Alloy does not guarantee traffic, earnings, account recovery, or appeal outcomes.
  • Complex account or policy issues are reviewed by human team members.
  • Personal information is handled according to the Privacy Policy.
